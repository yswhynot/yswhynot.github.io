Crescendo
Crescendo
Jan. 2017
MIT Entrepreneurship and Maker Skills Integrator, Hong Kong

The MEMSI program is a two week joint program of MIT and Hong Kong. There are 14 students from MIT and 16 students from Hong Kong in this January cohort. Our team includes 4 students from MIT (Caroline Jaffe, Shraman Ray Chaudhuri, Krithika Swaminathan, Tomi Adelusi) and 2 from Hong Kong (Stanley Ying, Yi Sha). Our team mainly focus on personal safety and sexual assault.

We propose a wearable device to enpower people to navigate uncomfortable social situations by creating a safe and connected environment.

Crescendo works by pressing the button on the wrist band and this will inform the group of friends. The wrist band is connected with mobile phone via bluetooth, and the phone will localize and navigate the user to the destination. I'm responsible for the Wi-Fi localization with find (both server and client) and also the bluetooth communication on Android application.
