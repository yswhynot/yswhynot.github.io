Smart POS
Smart POS
Oct. 2015
Cyberport Micro Fund, Hong Kong

Built the Smart POS project Android application. We propose a smart POS system that serves the item check out process in convenience stores and supermarkets. Customers are able to do self-payment with their own mobile phone application so that they do not need to wait in the long queue to check out their items.
