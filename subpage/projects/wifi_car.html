Wi-Fi Controlled Model Car

Developed Android-based remote-controlled model car. Responsible for PCB design and fabrication, MCU programming with MPLAB, final demonstration website design, Android application. Assisted with the casing design using Solidworks.

We call our model car Syzz Tank, as the short hand of the names our four team members. The functionalities of the Syzz Tank include:

  • Move around according to the instructions from the Android phone
  • LED lights indicating moving direction
  • Detect obstacles in front and stop/resume its movement accordingly.
